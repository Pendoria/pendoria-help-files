Puls3

Puls3 registered and starting playing on day one of Pendoria's release.

Ethereal

On June 12th of 2017, Puls3 created the guild named Ethereal. It would go on to rise through the rankings and be a premier guild for years to come.

Around 2019 Ethereal managed to gain a top three spot, and by 2020 they were fighting to become number one.

In 2022 Ethereal passed Scrapyard and became the number one tradeskill guild in the game.

Creations

Puls3 created an official website for tracking market flippers, a few years later they also developed a userscript to highlight said market flippers when viewed on the market.

Puls3 maintained a public web page with calculators for tradeskillers, as well as a ranking keeping track of the top twenty tradeskill and hybrid guilds. This service, named 'TS Master Race', would later be taken offline.

Puls3 created a popular userscript known as Pendorian Elite UI .

Staff History

After over a year of activity, Puls3 was invited to the moderation team. Once former developer Maria resigned, Puls3 was then promoted to help work on Pendoria's development.
